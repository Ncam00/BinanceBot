# ✅ Trade Entry Checklist (V1)

---

## 📊 MUST PASS ALL CONDITIONS

### 1. Market Structure

* [ ] Clear trend OR range identified
* [ ] Price not in middle (no-trade zone)

---

### 2. Pullback / Entry Condition

* [ ] Price pulled back from recent move
* [ ] Support or resistance level identified
* [ ] Price reacting at that level

---

### 3. Confirmation

* [ ] Bounce or rejection visible
* [ ] Candle confirms direction
* [ ] Not entering during strong impulse move

---

### 4. Indicators

* [ ] RSI not overbought (>70) for longs
* [ ] RSI not oversold (<30) for shorts
* [ ] Momentum aligns with direction

---

### 5. Risk Management

* [ ] Risk-to-reward ≥ 1:2
* [ ] Stop loss clearly defined
* [ ] Position size calculated correctly

---

## ❌ AUTO-REJECT CONDITIONS

* [ ] Price pumped recently (chasing)
* [ ] No clear support/resistance
* [ ] Mid-range chop
* [ ] Unclear structure

---

## 🎯 FINAL RULE

> If ANY condition fails → NO TRADE
