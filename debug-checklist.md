# 🛠️ Debug Checklist (V1)

---

## ❓ Why did the bot NOT take a trade?

Check:

* [ ] Was price in no-trade zone?
* [ ] Was there a real pullback?
* [ ] Was support/resistance clear?
* [ ] Did indicators align?
* [ ] Was RR ≥ 1:2?

---

## ❓ Why DID the bot take a trade?

Check:

* [ ] All checklist conditions passed?
* [ ] Entry was after confirmation?
* [ ] Stop loss valid?
* [ ] Not chasing?

---

## ❓ Bad trade? Find the cause:

* [ ] Entered mid-range?
* [ ] Weak support/resistance?
* [ ] RSI too high/low?
* [ ] Market conditions choppy?

---

## 🎯 Goal

> Every trade must be explainable
